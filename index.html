💡 Coping Tips

  • Take deep breaths for a few minutes
  • Call or message a friend
  • Try progressive muscle relaxation
  • Listen to music that makes you feel good

🧩 Simple Solutions to Common Feelings

Feeling anxious? Try grounding exercises like "5 things you see, 4 things you feel, 3 you hear..."

Feeling sad? Go outside for fresh air and journal your feelings.

Feeling tired? Drink water, stretch, or take a short nap.

Feeling overwhelmed? Write a to-do list and prioritize just one task.
